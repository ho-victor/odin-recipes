Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is like a delicious and cheesy tower of power, with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and gooey cheese that all come together to make your taste buds dance. It's basically like a savory cake, but instead of frosting, you get a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top. It's perfect for when you want to carb-load like a champion and indulge in some cheesy goodness. So go ahead, grab a fork and get ready to dive into a saucy, cheesy, and oh-so-satisfying slice of lasagna!

Ingredients

  1. 12 lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  3. 1 onion, chopped
  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  6. 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  7. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  8. 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  9. 1 teaspoon salt
  10. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  11. 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
  12. 1 egg, beaten
  13. 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  14. 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  15. 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Note that these ingredients are listed in the order in which they would typically be used in the recipe. You may also want to include additional ingredients, such as spinach or mushrooms, depending on your personal preference.
